About Us

Karimata was born from a love for coastline flavors and family recipes. Our chefs travel to coastal markets daily to source the freshest fish and shellfish. We blend Indonesian tradition with contemporary plating and Asian techniques.

Our Mission

  • Serve sustainably sourced seafood with respect to local fishing communities.
  • Deliver authentic Indonesian & Asian flavors with modern culinary craft.
  • Create warm, memorable dining experiences for families and food lovers.
